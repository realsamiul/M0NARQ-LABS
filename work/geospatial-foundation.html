Geospatial Foundation Model

Real-time Earth Observation

Client

Government of Bangladesh

Sector

Disaster Response

Timeline

6 months (2024)

Status

Production

Overview

Sentinel-1 SAR + Sentinel-2 optical fusion trained on 2M images of Brahmaputra basin. U-Net++ architecture with physics layers that enforce hydrology constraints. Deployed on edge devices for offline inference during connectivity loss.

The Challenge

GDACS flood maps arrived 72 hours after Sylhet floods. Relief coordination relied on rumours. Government needed sub-4-hour flood extent maps with 90%+ accuracy to dispatch aid.

The Solution

Pre-trained foundation model on NASA HLS + ESA SAR archives (2M images). Fine-tuned U-Net++ with SRTM slope constraints (water doesn't climb hills). Deployed quantised INT8 models on Raspberry Pi 4B for offline edge inference.
