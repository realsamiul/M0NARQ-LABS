Healthcare Multimodal AI

Document Intelligence & Drug Discovery

Client

Major Dhaka hospitals

Sector

Healthcare

Timeline

7 months (2024)

Status

Production (pilot phase)

Overview

OCR + NER pipeline extracts structured data from handwritten Bangla medical records. Multimodal model fuses patient history, lab results, and weather data for dengue outbreak prediction (9.8% error rate).

The Challenge

80% of Bangladesh hospital records are handwritten Bangla. Manual digitisation takes 12 days per 1,000 records. Dengue outbreaks predicted with 36% error rate (baseline model).

The Solution

Fine-tuned LayoutLM-v3 on 50K handwritten Bangla medical records. Multimodal fusion of patient history + lab results + weather (LSTM). Deployed on hospital edge servers (offline-first).

Impact

  • 9.8%
    Dengue forecast error rate
  • 26pp
    More accurate than baseline
  • <2 days
    Record digitization time
