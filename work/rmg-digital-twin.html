RMG Digital Twin

Industrial IoT & Predictive Maintenance

Client

Major RMG exporters

Sector

Manufacturing

Timeline

8 months (2024)

Status

Production (3 facilities)

Overview

Shop-floor IoT sensors (vibration, temperature, power draw) feed into gradient boosting models (CatBoost). Federated training across 3 factories aggregates insights without exposing proprietary data. Edge deployment on Jetson Nano.

The Challenge

Bangladesh RMG factories lose $180M annually to unplanned downtime. Manual inspection schedules miss 63% of failures. Factories won't send shop-floor data to cloud (IP concerns).

The Solution

Deployed 500 edge sensors per facility (vibration, thermal, power). Trained CatBoost models with federated learning (Paillier encryption). Edge inference on Jetson Nano (4GB). Alerts 6 hours before critical failures.

Impact

  • 34%
    Downtime reduction
  • $2.1M
    Savings per facility/year
  • 6h
    Failure prediction lead time
