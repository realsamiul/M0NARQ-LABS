The Physics

Water must flow downhill; vegetation always reflects NIR. We embed those laws as loss constraints, so the model sees events, not pixels.

Every false positive pays a penalty proportional to the local slope. Every missed flood pixel that meets backscatter + NDWI triggers a bigger penalty. The network learns hydrology without a hydrologist in the room. 

loss = CE(y_pred, y_physics) \
     + λ_slope * ReLU(slope>0.05 & y_pred==flood) \
     + λ_sig  * ReLU(vv>0.2 & y_pred==flood)
