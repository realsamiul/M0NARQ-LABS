The Stack: Foundation models. Federated training. Edge inference.

Physics-informed neural nets fine-tuned for emerging-market constraints. All numbers trace to production.

Four Pillars ⋯ One Decision Loop

1 · Data Gravity

We mirror Sentinel, VIIRS, open weather and customs records to a local STAC server—no round-trip to US regions.

2 · Physics-Bound Models

Loss functions penalise violation of hydrology, epidemiology or market-clearing equations. ML that knows the laws of the land generalises with fewer samples.

3 · Edge + Federation

Raspberry Pi, Coral TPU or even feature-phones push encrypted gradients; headquarters only sees statistics, never data.

4 · Action Interfaces

Instead of dashboards we send GeoJSON, SMS, MQTT—anything that plugs into real workflows.

The Engines that Power Everything

Hawkeye ▸ Geospatial AI

  • 2 core models: SegFormer-B2 floods, SimSiam crops; adding Swin-v2 deforestation Q4.
  • Max AOI processed so far: 48 000 km² (Bangladesh + Assam combined) in 2.4 hours GPU time.
  • Sentinel latency: 5–6 days → 30 min; goal: NRT stream to 3 min via AWS Ground Station.

Hyperion ▸ Time-Series AI

  • Ensemble factory: Ridge, CatBoost, XGBoost, TFT.
  • Pipeline auto-re-trains nightly using Prefect DAG & DVC tags.
  • Feature store holds 9.7 million rows, 36 features each.
